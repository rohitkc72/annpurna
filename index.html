Winter Trips

Get started September to November (Winter Trips) Autumn season – September to November is the most stable weather season in Nepal, characterised by crystal clear blue skies and very little precipitation.

Get started March to May (Spring Trips) Spring season – March to May is the second-best trekking season in Nepal with long daylight period.

Summer Trips

Get started June to August (Summer Trips) Summer season – June to August is monsoon season of Nepal, the most important natural phenomena to recharge the mountains, hills to terrains, feeding with a tremendous amount of snow and rainwater.
